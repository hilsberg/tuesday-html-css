Andrew Hilsberg, Digital Marketer

Andrew Hilsberg builds things: brands, communities, relationships and revenue. Drawing on a diverse marketing and sales background in music, media and sports, Andrew thrives on becoming a trusted advisor and consultant for his clients.

He's helped Jason Mraz become a household name, launched the Time Out brand into a major player on the Chicago cultural scene, and he conceived and built Cubs Fan Report, a daily email newsletter that curated the important news of the day for Cubs fans.

Andrew is forever fascinated by new technology, social media conversations, and what motivates consumers to take action.
